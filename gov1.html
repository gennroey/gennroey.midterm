QUALITY OBJECTIVES

  1. To promote progressive liberalization in the aviation industry;
  2. To comply with relevant regulatory standards and requirements;
  3. To meet all commitments to customers on time;
  4. To continuously improve and update our operational system, procedures and services rendered to our stakeholders, customers, and the general public;
  5. To improve the system for validating the data, information and documents relative to regulated entities in the Philippine air transport industry;
  6. To continuously improve and develop staff competency, and;
  7. To regularly conduct management review for effective implementation of the QMS in accordance with ISO 9001.
